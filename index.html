👋 The story behind this Github repo

🚀 Someone asked my past samples of work, and I was too lazy to create a domain or subdomain page for this, host it on a server, and send the link. Sending a portfolio by email, xls sheet, Word doc, Dropbox, or GDrive link is passé, ancient, and stone-aged. So, GitHub Pages came to my mind as a rescue option, live in 15 minutes flat. 🎨💻✨
